Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly are vendor invoices processed?

Vendor invoices are typically processed on a daily or 24-hour cadence, ensuring your AP is always up to date and your accountant has real-time visibility into liabilities.

Does your team work directly in our existing systems?

Yes, our dedicated pods log directly into your existing POS systems, back-office software, and accounting platforms to maintain your current technology stack without disruption.

How do you handle exceptions or discrepancies?

We establish clear escalation protocols during onboarding. Any invoice discrepancy or operational exception is flagged and escalated directly to your designated manager for quick resolution.

What types of reporting do you provide?

We generate comprehensive daily, weekly, and monthly operating reports tailored to your key metrics—from store-level P&L and labor variance to shrink and margin analysis.

Can you support both independent and franchise locations?

Yes. We work with independent operators as well as multi-unit franchisees, ensuring all reporting complies with specific franchisor chart of accounts and operational standards.
